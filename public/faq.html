How Can We Assist You?

Expert answers on stone durability, custom engraving times, and international shipping from Karachi's trusted legacy makers.

1

Durable Stones for Karachi?

For gravestones in Karachi, we highly recommend Black Granite or Ziarat White marble. These are naturally resistant to harsh sunlight and coastal humidity.

کراچی کے لیے بہترین پتھر؟ زیارت وائٹ اور بلیک گرینائٹ بہترین ہیں۔
2

Time for Custom Headstones?

Typically, a standard headstone takes 7 to 10 working days. For complex laser-engraved designs, it may take 15 to 20 days.

کتنا وقت لگتا ہے؟ عام طور پر 7 سے 10 دن۔
3

International Shipping?

Yes, we provide expert international shipping with secure wooden crate packaging. We have exported to the UK, USA, UAE, and Africa.

بین الاقوامی شپنگ؟ جی ہاں، ہم پوری دنیا میں برآمد کرتے ہیں۔
4

Payment Methods?

We accept Bank Transfers, Western Union, and secure online links. A 50% deposit is required to start the crafting process.

ادائیگی کا طریقہ؟ بینک ٹرانسفر اور آن لائن لنک۔
